Our Legacy

A Heritage of
Extraordinary Taste

Born in the heart of Kolkata, Nandanik has spent over seven years perfecting the art of authentic culinary tradition. Each dish is a love letter to our rich cultural heritage — crafted with hand-picked spices, time-honoured recipes, and an unwavering commitment to excellence.

From intimate gatherings to grand weddings of hundreds, we bring the same passion, precision, and royal experience to every table we serve.
